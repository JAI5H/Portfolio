About me

I’m a UI/UX Designer & Mentor with 2+ years of experience in designing user-centered digital products for startups, freelance clients, and hackathon teams. I translate user research and business goals into intuitive and accessible interfaces. My process covers research, personas, wireframing, prototyping, usability testing, and design systems.

With a background in Computer & Information Sciences, I bridge design and development. As a UI/UX Mentor, I guide junior designers, review their work, and help them build strong design thinking and practical skills. I’m passionate about creating usable, scalable, and impactful experiences that serve both users and business goals.
